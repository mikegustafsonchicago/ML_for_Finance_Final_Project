Wind Speed and Direction Analysis

Full Year Analysis

The full-year plots below show wind speed patterns across different years for each airport. Each subplot represents a single year, allowing for clear comparison of seasonal patterns and year-to-year variations. The x-axis spans the entire year (January through December), while the y-axis shows wind speed measurements.

Weekly Analysis

The weekly plots focus on a specific time period (April 1-7) to examine short-term wind patterns in detail. Each subplot shows both wind speed (y-axis) and wind direction (arrow orientation) for a single year. The arrows indicate wind direction, with their length being constant to avoid visual clutter. The thin connecting lines help track wind speed trends throughout the week.

Key Observations

  • Seasonal patterns in wind speed and direction can be observed in the full-year plots
  • The weekly plots reveal daily variations and potential diurnal patterns
  • Wind direction arrows help identify prevailing wind patterns and their changes
  • Year-to-year comparisons show both consistent patterns and notable variations
